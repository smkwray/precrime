# Lowest Likelihood to Be Rearrested — Trait Profiles

Cross-dataset comparison of traits associated with the **lowest observed rearrest / reincarceration rates**. All rates are unadjusted (raw subgroup rates, not model-predicted). Traits are ranked by how far below the base rate they fall.

> **Caveat:** These are descriptive associations, not causal claims. Rates are not adjusted for confounders. Small subgroups may produce unstable estimates.
